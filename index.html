The Farm Ecosystem

Large Scale, Natural Growth

Spanning over 50 acres of freshwater ponds, our farm utilizes an integrated ecosystem where Makhana (Fox Nuts) and Fish thrive together. The large lotus leaves of the Makhana provide shade for the fish, creating a symbiotic environment that ensures premium quality for both harvests.

  • Natural Pond Filtration
  • Organic Makhana Cultivation
